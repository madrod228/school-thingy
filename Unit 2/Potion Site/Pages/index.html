Potion Riddle

Setting the Scene

You are almost finished with your quest. You come upon a room with 6 different sized bottles. Each is filled with a different potion. They are arranged from smallest to largest. You know you need to move forward, but do not see any doors. Upon further inspection, you see a peice of paper with some instructions.

The Instructions

One among us will let you move ahead.

Another will transport the drinker back instead.

Two among our number hold only nettle wine.

Two of us are killers,waiting hidden in line

The Clues

  1. First, however slyly the poison tries to hide, You will always find some on nettle wine’s left side;
  2. Second, different are those who stand at either end, But if you would move onwards, neither is your friend;
  3. Third, as you see clearly, all are different size, Neither dwarf nor giant holds death in their insides;
  4. Fourth, the second left and the second on the right Are twins once you taste them, though different at first sight.
  5. Finally, if you won’t move forward and decide that you are done, There is enough in it’s container to help only one.
